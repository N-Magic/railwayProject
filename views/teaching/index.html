Meet Your Teachers

Dr. Nate Martin and Dr. Jaden Factor teach at Ozark High School, where they bring their doctorates in Fancy Coding Stuff and Architecture to the classroom. They lead the incredible AP Freakonomics, Market Fraud 101, Coffee Robotics, IB Sarcasm, 3D Printing, and the best of all – The Art of Subtlety.

They’re on a mission to make sure their students learn more than just the basics; they teach skills to navigate a world of complexity, nuance, and, of course, cutting-edge technology. The classes they offer are as unique and entertaining as their teaching style!
