NYLT

National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT)

BSA National Youth Leadership Training (NYLT) is an exciting, hands-on course that helps Scouts develop essential leadership skills through real-world challenges. Focused on teamwork, communication, and decision-making, NYLT empowers Scouts to become confident, effective leaders in their Troops and communities.

What to expect at NYLT

  • First-hand experience leading other around you.
  • Lifelong bonds, friendships, and memories.
  • An interactive leading experience with challenges and opportunities to grow.
    • A great time!
